Welcome to the Swip home page!

This site is a showcase of the SWIP system. This system and the underlying approach are described in various international scientific plublications:

  • C. Pradel, O. Haemmerlé and N. Hernandez. Expressing conceptual graph queries from patterns: how to take into account the relations. In Proceedings of the 19th International Conference on Conceptual Structures, ICCS'11, Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence #6828, pages 234-247, Derby, GB, July 2011. Springer.
  • Camille Pradel, Ollivier Haemmerlé and Nathalie Hernandez. A semantic web interface using patterns: the SWIP system. In Proceedings of GKR 2011, pages 172-187, Barcelona, Spain, July 2011. Croitoru et al.
  • Camille Pradel. Allowing end users to query graph-based knowledge bases. In Knowledge Engineering and Knowledge Management, page 8-15, Galway City, Ireland, October 2012.
  • Fabien Amarger, Ollivier Haemmerlé, Nathalie Hernandez, and Camille Pradel. Taking SPARQL 1.1 extensions into account in the SWIP system. In Conceptual Structures for STEM Research and Education, page 75-89, Bombay, Inde, 2013. Springer.
  • Camille Pradel, Guillaume Peyet, Ollivier Haemmerlé, Nathalie Hernandez: Swip at qald-3: results, criticisms and lesson learned (working notes). In CLEF 2013, Valencia, Spain, 23/09/2013-26/09/2013 (2013)
